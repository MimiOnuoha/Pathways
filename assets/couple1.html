This is the story of a couple in a long-distance relationship, split between the United Kingdom and the United States. One member of the group was based in London, the other in Boston. They had been dating each other for over a year when they agreed to share the data from their phones with me.

As someone who has had to navigate the difficulties of a long-distance relationship, I was interested in what the story the data from their phones would tell about what love looks like unfolded across borders. I collected their geolocation data, but also the metadata for their messages. In other words, not just where they went, but also information around when, where, and how they regularly communicated with each other.

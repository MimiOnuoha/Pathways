The Story of Three Roommates

This is the story of three friends and roommates


...in their last month of living together.


This is the story of the data of a goodbye.

Life Is Mundane


I tracked the roommates for nearly a month, and the biggest thing that emerged was the mundanity of everyday life.


Their map shows the roommates going to work, coming home, and going out.


Conclusion: the impending farewell didn't alter their everyday life.

Goodbyes Are Different


The fact that their geolocation data didn't easily represent the emotional experience of their goodbye doesn't render it unimportant.

In fact, on Roommate 2's final weekend in London, all three of the roommates spent substantially more of their time together.


On the map, this is the final weekend that they were tracked.

Goodbyes are hard to say, and harder to track.


But so are many of the most important things in life.

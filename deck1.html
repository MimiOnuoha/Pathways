There are 7.9 billion mobile-connected phones on the earth right now, and you probably have at least one of them.

Your phone is constantly tracking and storing your data.

And every day it gathers and produces more and more of that data.

I started to become curious about what that mobile data says about us.
Can it tell us anything about ourselves and our relationships with the people we love?

So I decided to investigate.

I found four groups of people who agreed to help me find answers by sharing data from their phones with me .

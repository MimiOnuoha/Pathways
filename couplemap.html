Logistics of Long-Distance

If you've ever been in a long-distance relationship, you know how important (and difficult) it is to stay in touch. In addition to their location information, this couple gave me all of their message metadata for the month that they participated in the project. Metadata refers to the information about the messages they sent to each other rather than the messages themselves, things like what times they were communicated and through what channels.

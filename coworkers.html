The Story of Three Co-Workers

This is the story of three co-workers who met while working at the same agency.


I collected their data from November to December 2014, hoping to see if there was anything that I could learn about their friendship.

Lesson 1: The Expected


Location-wise, the coworker's friendship was centered around their workplace.


(The grey marker shows their workplace, each color represents a different person's geolocation trail.)

Lesson 2: The Unexpected


The more I looked at their data, the more I noticed how two of the co-workers seemed to spend more time with each other than with the third.

Look for the overlap between the two colors (each one represents a different person's location trail).

Months later, the coworkers whose data showed they spent the most time together moved in together.


When I suggested that the data had hinted at their closeness, they were dismissive.


(But you should look at the data yourself and decide.)

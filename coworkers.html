The Story of Three Co-Workers

The three co-workers met while working at the same agency in East London.

I collected their data from November to December 2014, hoping to see if there was anything that I could learn about their friendship.

Lesson 1: The Expected


The coworker's friendship was definitely centered around their workplace.

Lesson 2: The Unexpected


The more I looked at their data, the more I noticed how two of the co-workers seemed to spend more time with each other than with the third.

Months after I had collected their data, the coworkers whose data showed they spent the most time together ended up moving in together.


When I brought up the fact that the data had suggested this might happen, they were dismissive. (But you should look at the data yourself to decide.)

This is the story of three coworkers

.

who met working at the same agency in East London.

I collected their data from November to December 2014, hoping to see if there was anything that I could learn about their friendship.

Lesson 1: The Expected


The coworker's friendship was definitely centered around their workplace.

Lesson 2: The Not-So-Expected


Two of the co-workers spent more time with each other than with the third.

When I questioned them about if the two of them really were closer than the other two, they were dismissive.

"That was just that month", one of the them even told me.

But you can look at the data yourself to decide.
